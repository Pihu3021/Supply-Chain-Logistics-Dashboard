3. Project Descripation

 This project focuses on building a comprehensive Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard for Samsung using Power BI. The dashboard provides end-to-end visibility into supplier operations, inventory management, shipment tracking, and customer sales performance. It helps identify operational bottlenecks, monitor KPIs, and generate actionable insights for improving supply chain efficiency and profitability.

4. Purpose

The purpose of this dashboard is to monitor and optimize supply chain operations by analyzing supplier performance, inventory levels, shipment activities, and customer sales trends. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making to improve operational efficiency, reduce delays, optimize inventory, and enhance customer satisfaction.

5.Key Features

# Business Problem Addressed
Large-scale supply chain operations involve complex processes such as supplier management, inventory tracking, shipment monitoring, and customer demand forecasting. Lack of centralized visibility can lead to shipment delays, stock shortages, increased operational costs, and reduced customer satisfaction.

# Goal of the Dashboard
Provide a centralized analytics platform for supply chain monitoring
Track operational KPIs across suppliers, inventory, shipments, and customers
Identify delays, inventory risks, and performance gaps
Improve business efficiency using interactive data visualizations

7.Key Insights

# Inventory Insights
High inventory stock observed for flagship products like Galaxy S24 Ultra and Galaxy Buds
Certain products show higher defect rates, indicating quality improvement opportunities
Inventory value peaked during Q4, reflecting seasonal demand growth

# Shipment Insights
Majority shipments were successfully delivered with a delivery rate above 75%
Carrier capacity and documentation issues were major causes of shipment delays
XPO Logistics and UPS Worldwide handled the highest shipment volumes

# Supplier Insights
Taiwan and South Korea suppliers showed the highest quality scores
Average supplier lead time remained stable across major suppliers
Supplier unit costs varied significantly, impacting procurement expenses

# Customer & Revenue Insights
Online sales channels generated the highest revenue contribution
Amazon and Flipkart were among the top revenue-generating customers
Smartphones contributed the highest sales volume among all product categories
